01 · Foundations

Color Tokens

The palette is warm, restrained, and editorial. No teal. No gradients. Cream whites and near-blacks carry the weight; deep navy is the only accent. All surfaces use the warm white family — never pure #ffffff.

Black --black #0d0d0b Dark section backgrounds, nav (scrolled), footer
White --white #f6f5f1 Primary page background, card backgrounds
Off-White --off #efede8 Alternating sections, hover states, table rows
Ink --ink #1e1e1c Body text, headings on light backgrounds
Mid --mid #888680 Secondary text, captions, eyebrows on light
Rule --rule #dddad3 Grid gaps, dividers, borders, ghost numerals
Navy --navy #1a2744 CTA section background, dark accent blocks
Accent --accent #2a4080 Tags, labels, hover bars, links — THE only accent
Never use: teal, cyan, purple, green, gold, or any saturated color as an accent. Never use pure white (#ffffff) or pure black (#000000). The warm white family maintains the editorial luxury feel that signals premium positioning to director-level and above audiences.

02 · Typography

Type System

Two fonts only. EB Garamond for editorial display moments — large headings and block quotes — always at weight 400, often italic. Outfit for everything functional: navigation, body, eyebrows, buttons, labels. Never swap these roles.

03 · Spacing

04 · Interaction

07 · Rules

Design Rules

RuleRequirement
Border radiusAlways 0. No rounded corners anywhere.
Grid gapsAlways 2px. Set with gap:2px + background:var(--rule) on parent.
Accent color--accent (#2a4080) is the only permitted accent. No teal, green, gold, purple.
Serif usageEB Garamond for h2, h3, pull quotes, stat numbers only. Never for h2/h3 in all-caps.
Uppercase textOnly in Outfit, never in EB Garamond.
Hover — cardsbackground → --off. Accent bar scaleX(0→1) on ::after.
Hover — buttonsopacity .75 + translateY(-1px). No color/bg change.
Reveal animationopacity 0→1 + translateY(22px→0), .75s ease. Use IntersectionObserver.
NavTransparent until scroll > 60px, then background: rgba(13,13,11,.94) + backdrop-filter: blur(12px).
FontsEB Garamond + Outfit only. No third font.
BackgroundsNever pure #fff or #000. Always --white (#f6f5f1) or --black (#0d0d0b).
Section rhythmAlternate white → off-white → dark → white. Max one dark section per page.
Emoji / iconsNone. Use typographic marks (—, ·, →) or SVG only if truly needed.
